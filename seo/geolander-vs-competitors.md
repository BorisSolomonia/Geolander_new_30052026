## Geolander: tourist-focused, accountable, mountain-ready

Geolander is built around one idea: **a tourist exploring Georgia should deal with one company that takes responsibility for the whole trip** — not a faceless marketplace and not a supplier you've never heard of.

**What makes Geolander different:**

- **Free delivery to Tbilisi International Airport (TBS)** or your hotel — you land, your car is waiting.
- **A 4x4 fleet chosen for Georgian roads.** The best parts of Georgia — Kazbegi, Svaneti, Tusheti, the Kakheti wine country — need a capable vehicle. Geolander's fleet is selected for these routes, not just city driving.
- **24/7 support in English and Georgian.** Real people, fast WhatsApp replies, roadside assistance if anything goes wrong.
- **No hidden fees.** Basic insurance is included; the price you're quoted is the price you pay.
- **Local expertise.** Geolander doesn't just hand over keys — it helps tourists plan where to go and what car fits the route.

**Best for:** International tourists who want a smooth, supported, end-to-end rental experience and plan to drive into the mountains.

## Local Rent: a marketplace of many suppliers

Local Rent (localrent.com) is an **aggregator** — it lists cars from many independent local suppliers across Georgia and lets you compare them in one place.

**Strengths:** Wide selection, easy price comparison, established platform with many reviews.

**Trade-offs:** You're not renting from Local Rent itself — you're renting from whichever individual supplier owns that listing. Service quality, delivery, and support **vary by supplier**, and accountability is split between the platform and the operator.

**Best for:** Experienced travelers who are comfortable vetting individual suppliers and want to compare many options at once.

## Premium Auto Rent: premium vehicles, direct booking

Premium Auto Rent (premiumautorent-ge.com) is a direct rental company positioned around **premium and business-class vehicles**.

**Strengths:** Focus on higher-end cars; you deal with one company.

**Trade-offs:** Less emphasis on the tourist/adventure use case and mountain-ready 4x4s; check delivery and support terms per booking.

**Best for:** Travelers who specifically want a premium sedan for city and business use.
