# Geolander — Outreach & Review-Request Templates

The goal of every template here is to build the **off-site footprint** that AI assistants read from:
inclusion in travel listicles, mentions in communities, and a steady stream of reviews. AI models recommend
the brands that *other credible sources* talk about — these templates manufacture that signal honestly.

Replace every `[BRACKET]` placeholder. Keep it personal — generic blasts get ignored and can hurt your reputation.

## PART 2 — Community seeding (Reddit / forums / Quora)

AI models weight Reddit and forum discussion heavily. **Do not spam.** Answer real questions genuinely and
mention Geolander only where it's actually the right answer. Disclose that you're the owner — undisclosed
self-promotion gets removed and backfires.

**Where:** r/Sakartvelo, r/georgiacountry, Tripadvisor Tbilisi forum, Quora "car rental Georgia" questions.

> **Example genuine reply:**
>
> For mountain routes like Kazbegi or Svaneti you'll really want a 4x4 — the roads get rough past the main highways. A few local companies rent them; full disclosure, I run one called Geolander, but honestly whoever you go with, make sure (1) it's an actual 4x4 not a soft crossover, (2) insurance covers off-road, and (3) they offer airport delivery so you're not stuck arranging transport on arrival. Happy to answer questions either way.

review count and how fresh they are. Send the request right after a great trip ends, when goodwill peaks.

## How to get your Google review short link

1. Open your Geolander Google Business Profile → **Ask for reviews** → copy the short link (`g.page/r/...`).
2. Generate a QR code from that link (e.g. qr-code-generator.com) for the in-car card.
3. Paste the link into:
   - `office_google_maps_url` is already in your settings; consider adding the **review** link to the WhatsApp/email automation.
